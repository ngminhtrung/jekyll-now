EXPORTS

In 2015 Vietnam exported $185B, making it the 24th largest exporter in the world. During the last five years the exports of Vietnam have increased at an annualized rate of 18.6%, from $77.3B in 2010 to $185B in 2015. The most recent exports are led by Broadcasting Equipment which represent 16.3% of the total exports of Vietnam, followed by Integrated Circuits, which account for 5.81%.
